>

>
> ```bash

>   -H 'Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY' \

>         \"type\": \"product\",
>         \"title\": \"Wireless Headphones\",
>         \"content\": \"Premium noise-cancelling...\",
>         \"metadata\": {
>           \"sku\": \"WBH-001\",
>           \"price\": 199.99,

## Style Guide

### Visual Style
- **Resolution**: 1080p (1920x1080) minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 30fps or 60fps
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9
- **Branding**: Include RAG Assistant logo in corner
- **Color Scheme**: Match brand colors

### Audio Style
- **Tone**: Professional but friendly
- **Pace**: Moderate (not too fast, not too slow)
- **Volume**: Consistent levels throughout
- **Music**: Subtle background music, lower during speech
- **Sound Effects**: Minimal, only for emphasis

### On-Screen Text
- **Font**: Sans-serif (Arial, Helvetica, or brand font)
- **Size**: Large enough to read on mobile
- **Color**: High contrast with background
- **Duration**: Long enough to read comfortably
- **Animation**: Smooth, not distracting

### Callouts & Annotations
- **Arrows**: Use to point to specific UI elements
- **Boxes**: Highlight important areas
- **Zoom**: Zoom in on small text or details
- **Blur**: Blur sensitive information
- **Color**: Use brand colors consistently

## Analytics & Metrics

### Track These Metrics
- **Views**: Total and unique views
- **Watch Time**: Average percentage watched
- **Engagement**: Likes, comments, shares
- **Click-Through Rate**: On embedded links
- **Drop-Off Points**: Where viewers stop watching
- **Traffic Sources**: Where viewers come from
- **Demographics**: Audience location and device

### Success Criteria
- **Completion Rate**: >60% watch to end
- **Engagement Rate**: >5% like/comment
- **Conversion Rate**: >10% click documentation links
- **Satisfaction**: >4.5/5 average rating

### Optimization Based on Data
- Shorten videos with high drop-off rates
- Create follow-up content for popular topics
- Improve audio/visual quality based on feedback
- Update outdated content regularly
